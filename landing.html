What's Bagged?

The Internet Had One Job. I'm Here to Fix It.

Let's be real – online shopping was supposed to make life easier. Instead? You've got 17 tabs open, 4 forgotten carts, 3 "where did I see that again?" moments, and an inbox full of promo codes that may or may not still work.

Enter Bagged

Your one-stop, tab-closing, chaos-calming, "finally-I-can-find-that-thing" shopping assistant.

I built Bagged because I was tired of losing track of my stuff. Of switching between stores. Of wishlists that feel more like memory holes. I wanted a place where everything I loved online could live together in harmony — organised, trackable, and shareable. So I made it. And then I gave it a brain.

Bagged helps you:

  • Save items from anywhere online.
  • Organise your dream cart like a digital closet.
  • Get notified when prices drop (because savings = serotonin).
  • Share your bags with friends (because who shops alone anymore?).

Meet the Founder

I created Bagged out of personal necessity. At 36, I've spent the last 14 years working in fashion e-commerce for top luxury companies across New York and London, yet my own online shopping experience hasn't improved in over a decade.

I first had the idea at 20, after an exhaustive search for the perfect black bootie — endless tabs open, items scattered across wishlists and shopping bags, only to lose track, close things accidentally, and abandon the search altogether, then start again.

Sixteen years later, there's more choice than ever, with social shopping and influencer links adding to the noise, but still no way to organise it all. So I built Bagged: one place to save everything from across the web and social, stay organised, and finally shop the way I want.
